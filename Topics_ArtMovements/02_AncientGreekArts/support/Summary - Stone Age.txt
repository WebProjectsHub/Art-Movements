Stone Age Summary
Techniques to fire vases were first developed during the Early Neolithic Period.

Middle Neolithic saw the development of 'megaroid" also refered to as "megaron".

The megaron later developed into the hall for Greek palaces. 

The megaron's rectilinear shape would influence later developments of Greek temples.

The "tsangli" type of structure was also developed during the Neolithic Periods.
