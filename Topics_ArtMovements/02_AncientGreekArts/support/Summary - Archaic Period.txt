Category	Qualities
Sculpture	- Emergence of the “Archaic smile” on statues
		- Increased naturalism and attention to human anatomy
		- Development of large-scale marble sculptures
		- Introduction of kouros (male) and kore (female) statues
Pottery		- Transition from geometric designs to more naturalistic human figures
		- Use of black-figure and red-figure techniques
		- Depictions of mythological and everyday scenes
Architecture	- Development of the Doric and Ionic orders
		- Construction of large stone temples
		- Emphasis on harmonious proportions
Painting	- Vase paintings illustrating epic tales and human activities
		- Influence from Egyptian and Near Eastern art
Jewelry and Metalwork
		- Fine jewelry making and intricate metalwork
		- Use of motifs like palmettes, lotuses, and hybrid animals
